Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We've got answers. If you have some other questions, see our support center.

What is call tracking?

Call tracking gives you actionable reports of your business call activities. These reports include data on incoming, outgoing and missed calls, both agent and department-wise, and also shows peak calling hours, regional location of callers and more.

How is business call tracking beneficial?

With detailed call reports, agent and department wise performance on calls is monitored and follow-up on missed calls is done easily. Also, business call tracking gives an insight in the results of your marketing activities, considering the source of your incoming calls.

How does call tracking help in analyzing marketing ROI?

Different marketing campaigns are assigned different virtual numbers (calls are landed on the same phone numbers). With call reports of each virtual number, the number of calls from respective marketing campaign are checked and thus, your marketing ROI is better analyzed.

For how long call reports are available?

Business call reports are available on the panel for unlimited time duration. At any point of time, you can find previous call reports of any date range.

Are these reports downloadable?

Yes, these call reports can be checked and downloaded from the panel itself. Also, the call reports will be sent to you everyday via SMS and emails.
