Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We've got answers. If you have some other questions, see our support center.

What is click-to-call feature?

Click to call is an upgraded feature through which allows your team/agents to connect with the customer with just a click on the web-panel. A sever number is shown to the agent and customer while they are connecting with each other on call.

How does click-to-call work?

Add our click to call feature on your desktop. As you click on any number on the website, the number will be automatically dialed a call to your number. As you receive the call, this call will be placed to the customer. This feature will thus allow your team to call the customer without manually dialling the number on phone.

What are the benefits of click-to-call?

Click to call is an advanced feature that allows you to reach out to your customers by generating outbound calls. It enables you to follow up with those customers whose calls you missed when you were busy or unavailable to receive calls.
