Frequently Asked Questions

We are here to answer all your questions regarding the

What is the difference between Office IVR and Cloud call-center product plans?

VoiceTech can be used by different kinds of businesses as per their requirement and suitability. We have created products and pricing considering the requirements of different sizes of businesses. Office IVR is a product for businesses who want simple IVR solution along with the virtual mobile or tollfree number.Cloud call-center is a product designed for businesses seeking advanced solutions like CRM, API integrations and more.

How do I decide which plan to choose?

You can choose that based on your requirements and call volume: i) Micro & small businesses can use Office IVR plan as a simple IVR solution to route calls to various users and department, access logs and basic reports on calls. ii) Small and mid-sized businesses can choose Cloud call-center Desk plan to run a helpline or micro-small call center with active access to the user panel, CRM integration, etc. iii) Mid-large sized organizations can choose Cloud call-center Prime plan for complex or well-managed use cases with access to a large set of reports or high usage of API. iv) Enterprises can contact us for their customized and advanced solution

What is the refund policy?

We promise that we will provide a full refund in case you are not satisfied with the product, anytime within first 30 days for your service activation. Click here for the complete refund policy.

Which all CRMs can be integrated with the solution?

You can integrate any CRM with our call management system. Some of the most popular CRMs which can be easily integrated with VoiceTech are Pipedrive, ZOHO, Salezshark, Kreato and more.

Can I change my plan later?

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime as per your requirements and/or usage. You can do it yourself from your VoiceTech panel's billing section, or drop an email to support@myoperator.com with your contact and service details. We'll be happy to assist you.

What form of payment do you accept?

You can pay via credit card, debit card, net banking, UPI, wallet or cheque. You can also opt for recurring payments options via credit cards or netbanking. Currently, we offer only annual subscription.

How do I port my existing number?

We understand that you may already be having a contact number shared with your existing customers. You can easily port your existing tollfree or mobile numbers to VoiceTech provided number. Our sales representative will guide you through the process. Alternatively, you can go through the guidelines and video tutorials for porting your number to VoiceTech here.
