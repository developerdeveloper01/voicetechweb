What is the VoiceTech Free Forever Plan?

The “VoiceTech Free Forever Plan” is a mobile call tracking solution that allows you to monitor and track all your business’s calls 24*7, even when you’re not in office. All you need to do is visit the link and download the app on your mobile phone and on the devices of your employees.

Benefits of the VoiceTech Free Forever plan:
  • Track calls: Track all your business calls (department and user wise) to monitor your team’s performance from anywhere.
  • Follow up: Set reminders for the calls you or your team members have missed to reach back to your callers.
  • Team accountability: Monitor your agents performance over the calls by viewing the number of calls they receive, miss and follow up.
