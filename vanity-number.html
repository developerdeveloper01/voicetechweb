What is a vanity number?

Vanity number is an easy to remember virtual number with unique number sequence. The number can also spells your business name when written corresponding to the numeric digits. Example, 1800-MYOP-TR will be dialed as 1800-6967-87.

What are the features available with a vanity number?

Along with a vanity number you also get features like customer call recording, call tracking, IVR, remarketing SMS, call reports, callers’ database and more.
